Welcome to **xaashay.github.io** — a personal blog focused on FPGA design, Verilog HDL, digital logic, and hardware engineering.

This site documents notes, tutorials, and writeups on topics like clock domain crossing, synthesis, simulation, and building real digital systems from scratch.

## Background

I'm a student/enthusiast passionate about digital design and embedded systems. This site documents my learning journey — from blinking LEDs to building more complex digital circuits.

## Topics Covered

- Verilog & SystemVerilog design
- FPGA synthesis and implementation
- Clock domain crossing and timing
- Simulation with tools like Verilator and GTKWave
- Digital signal processing on FPGAs
